Tayo Plastic

Tayo Plastic Company is a growing manufacturing company located in Hargeisa, Somaliland. The company produces its own high-quality plastic products designed for everyday household and commercial use. Tayo focuses on durability, affordability, and local production, supporting the community by creating jobs and reducing reliance on imported plastic goods.
